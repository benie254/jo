Hello, I am Jo. Nice of you to visit!

Wanna know a little more about me?

My old man named me Jo. I am three years old. Among us canines, that's an average youth. I like to think of myself as sociable and playful. I have also packed some energy in my hinds and fores. It's why they call me the German Shepherd.

Jane

When I was two, I met this girl, Jane. I liked her. I still do. We do not see each other often, though. See, her folks let her out in the evenings; mine let me out in the morning. We always have only a little time before she is or I am locked up.

I hope to raise a family with Jane, someday. Someday.
